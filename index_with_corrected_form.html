Meet the Author

Adriane Williams

Adriane Williams is a passionate storyteller with a love for creating heartwarming tales that inspire children to embrace who they are. With a knack for rhymes and a playful imagination, Adriane brings characters like Percy the Peculiar Possum to life—encouraging young readers to celebrate their uniqueness and be proud of what makes them different. When she’s not writing, Adriane enjoys spending time with family and her two pets—a psycho kitty named Layla and a crazy puppy named Bella.
